Profile of Ian Kwan

Ian likes to dabble around.
Project to project, he relishes each initial planning stage and the stimulation his mind goes through.
Then he enjoys creating and bringing his ideas to life, with his own hands.

Jewellery Designing

For a little over a decade now, Ian has been designing fine jewellery professionally.

At work, he hand renders his designs with watercolour and ink.

Relevant skills in Ian's repertoire include Matrix CAD program, Silversmithing and Stonesetting.

You can find more of his work on Instagram @IanKHF.

Engraving

Ian picked up engraving during the lockdown of 2020.

He spent all that free time gathering all the necessary components and built his own home-made pneumatic-assist machine from this YouTube video. It was a wonderful learning experience!

Learning the online video tutorials he purchased online, Ian started engraving.

The process has a therapeutic effect on Ian and he wishes to do more of it in the future, when time permits.
